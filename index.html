The Bioscience Problem

                    

                        In intensive tropical livestock farming, feed costs represent up to 70% of operational overhead. Identifying the Inflection Point—the precise age where an animal's daily growth rate peaks and begins to slow down—is critical. 
                    

                    

                        Past this peak, feed conversion efficiency decreases rapidly. This app lets you track growth acceleration and deceleration by observing the derivative of the model.
                    

                


                

                    
Mathematical Models

                    
                    

                        
                        

                            f(x) = 320 / (1 + 20.33 * e-0.22x)
                        

                        Yields total cumulative body weight (kg) at month x.
                    


                    

                        
                        

                            f'(x) = 1431.23 * e-0.22x / (1 + 20.33 * e-0.22x)2
                        

                        Yields instantaneous weight gain rate (kg/month).
